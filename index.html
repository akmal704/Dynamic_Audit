Who We Are

Big 4 Methodology.
Local Market Mastery.

Dynamic Audit was founded on a simple conviction: businesses in Uzbekistan deserve the same quality of financial oversight that multinationals receive from the world's top audit firms — without paying multinational fees.

Our two founders trained and worked at EY and Deloitte, where they led audit engagements across energy, banking, agriculture, and trading sectors. When they returned to Uzbekistan, they brought the same rigorous methodology with them — calibrated for local regulations, tax law, and business culture.

Today Dynamic Audit serves companies ranging from fast-growing startups to established enterprises and state-linked entities. Whether you need a statutory audit, a financial clean-up, or a Big 4-grade CFO sitting alongside your management team, we deliver work that stands up to international scrutiny.
